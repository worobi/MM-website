Brand Guidelines

Logo, colors, typography & voice — everything you need to represent us consistently.

01 — Identity

The Logo

Clear Space

Always give the logo breathing room. Maintain a minimum clear zone equal to the height of the cookie icon on all four sides. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

Minimum clear zone on all sides
≥ 1× icon height

Minimum size: 60px wide on screen  |  0.75 inch in print

02 — Logo Usage

What Not To Do

✕ Don't Stretch

Never distort the logo's proportions horizontally or vertically.

✕ Don't Squish

Always scale uniformly — hold Shift when resizing.

✕ Don't Rotate

Keep the logo upright at all times.

✕ Don't Use Low-Contrast Backgrounds

Avoid placing the logo on backgrounds similar in color to the gradient.

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

🏠

Homemade & Warm

We speak like a neighbor, not a corporation. Friendly, genuine, and always welcoming.

🎉

Playful & Fun

Bakery should be joyful. Use light humor, exclamation points when earned, and never be stiff.

🏡

Community-First

We're Port Huron proud. Reference local landmarks, shout out the community, celebrate events.

✨

Honest & Simple

No marketing fluff. Real ingredients, real people, real baking. Let the product speak.

✓ Sound Like This

  • "Fresh out of the oven just for you 🍪"
  • "Baked in Port Huron, loved everywhere."
  • "Grab a dozen — they won't last long!"

✕ Not Like This

  • "Our artisanal confectioneries..."
  • "Leveraging premium baked goods solutions."
  • "Purchase our inventory-available SKUs."
