Deposits & Payments

💳 Deposits & Payments

A deposit is a partial payment that locks in your order. Our deposit scale is:

• Orders under $20 — $5 flat deposit
• Orders $20–$50 — $10 flat deposit
• Orders over $50 — 50% of the estimated total

The remaining balance is collected at pickup.
You can pay your deposit three ways at checkout:

• 💳 Card (Stripe) — Pay instantly and securely online. Your order is confirmed the moment payment clears.
• 💚 CashApp — Choose CashApp at checkout. After submitting your order, send the deposit to our $cashtag shown on the confirmation page.
• 💙 Venmo — Choose Venmo at checkout. After submitting your order, send the deposit to our Venmo username shown on the confirmation page.

Important for CashApp & Venmo: Your deposit must be sent within 24 hours of placing your order or your order will be automatically canceled.
The remaining balance is due at pickup. We accept cash, Venmo, CashApp, and card at pickup. We'll remind you of the exact total when we confirm your order.
Absolutely. Stripe is used by millions of businesses worldwide and is one of the most trusted payment processors available. Your card information is processed directly by Stripe — we never see or store your payment details on our end.
Deposits are refundable if you cancel with more than 48 hours notice before your scheduled pickup date. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable because we will have already begun baking your order. If something comes up, please reach out as soon as possible and we'll do our best to work with you.

About the Business

📋 Cottage Food & Legal

Michigan's Cottage Food Law allows individuals to produce and sell certain types of non-hazardous food items made in their home kitchen without requiring a commercial kitchen license. Products must be shelf-stable (no refrigeration required), properly labeled, and sold direct-to-consumer. Moni's Munchies operates in full compliance with this law.
