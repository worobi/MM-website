✦ Where It All Started

From Hobby to Community

Moni's Munchies started the way all great things do — with a love for baking and neighbors who kept asking for "just one more" cookie.

What began as weekend batches for family turned into something bigger when friends started placing requests, then coworkers, then the whole neighborhood. It wasn't long before Monica realized she wasn't just baking for fun anymore — she was building something.

Today, Moni's Munchies serves Port Huron and surrounding communities through farmers markets, local events, and direct orders. Every single item is still made by hand, in the same kitchen where it all started.

✦ What We Believe

Our Values

🏡

Homemade Always

No shortcuts. No preservatives. Every product is made from scratch in a real kitchen, by real hands, with real care. We believe you can taste the difference.

💜

Community First

We're not a faceless brand — we're your neighbors. We show up at local markets, take pride in Port Huron, and believe in supporting the people around us.

✨

Quality Over Quantity

We keep a tight, thoughtful menu so every item gets the attention it deserves. We'd rather do 10 things great than 50 things okay.

🎉

Fun & Family-Friendly

Baked goods are meant to bring people together. We're playful, approachable, and love seeing our cookies show up at birthday parties, family dinners, and farmers markets.
