Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

Moni's Munchies is a Michigan Cottage Food Operation owned and operated by Monica and Brandon Worobi, based in Port Huron, Michigan. We sell freshly baked goods directly to consumers through our website, farmers markets, and local events.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at orders@monismunchies.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name — to identify your order and address you properly.
  • Email address — to send order confirmations and respond to your messages.
  • Phone number — to coordinate pickup details if needed.
  • Pickup date & order details — to fulfill your order.
  • Payment information — card payments are processed securely by Stripe and never stored on our servers. Cash, CashApp, and Venmo payments are handled directly between you and us.

If you sign up for our newsletter, we also collect your email address and optionally your first name to send you updates about new products, market dates, and seasonal specials.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Communicate with you about your order, pickup, and any questions
  • Send marketing emails if you opted in (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Improve our products and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services to operate our business:

  • Stripe — secure card payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Mailchimp — newsletter delivery for customers who opt in.
  • Resend — transactional email for order notifications sent to us internally.

These services have their own privacy policies and security practices. We only share the minimum information necessary for them to perform their services.

5. Data Retention

We retain order information for accounting and record-keeping purposes consistent with Michigan business requirements. Newsletter subscriber data is held until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal browser storage (such as remembering if you dismissed the newsletter popup). We do not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics, or cross-site tracking cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, email us at orders@monismunchies.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
