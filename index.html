✦ Our Story

Baked with
Heart & Soul

Moni's Munchies started the way all great things do — with a love for baking and neighbors who kept asking for "just one more" cookie.

Monica Worobi is the heart and hands behind every batch. Based in Port Huron, MI, she brings homemade quality to every order — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just real baked love.

From farmers markets to custom orders, Moni's Munchies is a community-first brand built on one simple idea: everyone deserves a little something delicious.
